Why CALY

Calorie tracking should not feel like homework.

Traditional tracking asks people to separate every ingredient, weigh it, search a database, calculate nutrition, and repeat the entire process for every meal. That friction is exactly what CALY was built to remove.

The challenge

Traditional tracking asks too much from the user.

Every meal can turn into a long sequence of manual decisions before it is ever recorded.

01

Separate every food item and ingredient.

02

Estimate or measure each portion manually.

03

Search nutritional databases item by item.

04

Calculate calories and macronutrients repeatedly.

Three ways to log

Built around how people actually eat.

CALY supports different situations without forcing users into one rigid input method.

01

Take a photo

CALY separates the foods on the plate, estimates each portion, calculates nutritional values, and records the meal.

02

Describe your meal

Write naturally in Arabic, Egyptian dialect, Franco-Arabic, English, or mixed language. CALY structures the description into reliable food records.

03

Save meals you eat often and add them again in seconds — without repeating the full analysis workflow.

Inside the intelligence

Five systems. One seamless experience.

The interface is simple because the engineering behind it is not. Each meal moves through specialized AI, mathematical, and software layers.

Computer Vision

Recognizing every item — not just the plate.

Three fine-tuned YOLO instance-segmentation models run together. A custom Mask-IoU ensemble removes duplicates, protects nested toppings, resolves conflicts, and fills missed regions.

  • Approximately 40,000 images across 220 food classes
  • 46 Egyptian food classes added for regional coverage
  • Pixel-level masks for multiple foods in the same meal
